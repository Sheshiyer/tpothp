## Role in Tryambakam Noesis

**A Path of a Humble Psychic Plumber** is the first **Symbolic Narratives** release in the Tryambakam Noesis ecosystem.

It carries Noesis philosophy through story rather than explanation:

- Sovereignty over dependency
- Inhabitation over transcendence
- Questions over answers
- Integration over accumulation
- Difficulty as feature
- Meaning as authored, not delivered

## Core Premise

Pichet is a humble psychic plumber in a living bioscape city where anatomy, municipal infrastructure, sacred geometry, and consciousness are the same system viewed at different scales.

Each episode follows a repair job that makes one Selemene mirror felt as story. The repair does not deliver an answer, prophecy, or diagnosis. It gives the affected person enough symbolic clarity to witness a pattern and choose their next authorship move.

The older body-system/chakra structure remains useful visual mythology, but the current canon anchor is Selemene: 16 engines, 6 workflows, and the principle that the system succeeds when the person outgrows dependency on it.

## World

A living bioscape city where:

- streets pulse like arteries
- subway networks fire like neurons
- cathedrals grow as bone architecture
- water treatment facilities behave like kidneys
- atmospheric towers breathe and speak
- clocktowers metabolize time like liver ducts
- lymphatic canals filter emotion and psychic debris
- the city’s heart contains a fractured Sri Yantra seal

## Main Character

**Pichet**

A grounded, humble psychic plumber. He is not a guru, superhero, or chosen savior. He repairs blockages because that is the work in front of him.

Traits:

- practical, gentle, observant
- carries sacred tools like ordinary trade instruments
- speaks in simple repair metaphors
- understands that pain is not failure; it is information
- evolves from municipal repairman into conscious co-creator

## Antagonistic / Shadow Force

**The Null Weaver**

Not a villain in the simple sense. The Null Weaver is nihilism, solipsism, and separation pain crystallized into a system-corrupting intelligence. By the finale, he becomes guardian of healthy skepticism and necessary questioning.

## Visual Language

Use original anime-inspired cinematic language without copying protected IP.

- living anatomy + sacred geometry + municipal infrastructure
- manuscript/cartographic Noesis palette: Deep Ink, Bone, Aged Gold, Stone Grey, Terracotta
- painterly backgrounds, organic city textures, precise symbolic linework
- no direct imitation of Studio Ghibli or any copyrighted character style
- soft nostalgic atmosphere where appropriate, but grounded by civic/anatomical detail

## Selemene Story Rules

- Every episode is a mirror, not a method.
- No episode predicts fate or tells a character what to do.
- Pichet repairs conditions for witnessing; he does not perform salvation.
- A calculation, symbol, engine, or rhythm can appear, but only as an invitation to inquiry.
- The correct emotional endpoint is authorship: the character becomes more able to choose.
- The recurring doctrine line is: `Not prediction. Reflection. Inquiry. Witness.`
