# Episode Name Lab

Created: 2026-06-15
Status: candidate naming systems only. Do not replace canon titles until a direction is chosen.

## Naming Problem

The current direction is about 60% there: the Selemene map is right, Pichet is right, and the proof-of-world structure is right. The remaining weakness is title taste.

Some current names are vivid but too literal, medical, or genre-generic:

- `The Phantom Bladder` reads accidentally comic.
- `The Temporal Bile Duct` is conceptually accurate but clunky.
- `Gaia's Lymphatic Rebellion` feels broad and less Pichet-specific.
- `The Sri Yantra Rupture` is strong, but may reveal the finale too early.

## Naming Criteria

Good TPoTHP episode titles should:

- Feel like mythic repair tickets, not medical textbook chapters.
- Keep the humble Pichet / plumber frame alive.
- Avoid generic anime apocalypse phrasing.
- Avoid deterministic prophecy language.
- Preserve the Noesis rule: mirror, inquiry, witness, authorship.
- Work as X/video hooks without sounding like SaaS content.
- Be short enough to remember and strange enough to click.

## Recommended Direction: Mythic Repair Titles

This keeps the `The...` grammar, but makes each title more poetic and less anatomical.
