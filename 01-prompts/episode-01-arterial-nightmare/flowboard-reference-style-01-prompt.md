## Prompt

```text
Create one polished vertical anime production flowboard for an original symbolic narrative short titled "The Dream in the Artery".

REFERENCE USE
Use the two workflow reference images only for composition language and presentation discipline: large cinematic hero image at the top, clean production-board panels below, readable character/object/staging sections, strong whitespace, clear visual hierarchy, and polished anime workflow moodboard quality.
Do not copy their characters, clothing, logo, text, beach/countryside story, cat, wind chime, surfboard, brand marks, or any readable typography.

PRODUCTION PIPELINE INTENT
This board must be useful for the next AI animation step. Treat it as a motion-ready production reference: identity, setting, props, palette, and three story beats should be clear enough for a later Seedance prompt. Avoid decorative lore that cannot be animated.

PICHET IDENTITY
Use the Pichet reference images as strict identity anchors. Pichet must read as a mature South Asian working adult in his late 30s to early 40s: grounded, weathered, calm, practical, kind, repair-worker bearing. No child face. No teenage anime protagonist. No mascot proportions. No guru, superhero, monk, wizard, or action fighter.

PROJECT CONTEXT
This is Tryambakam Noesis / Selemene symbolic narrative onboarding. The story is not prediction or prophecy. It is reflection, inquiry, witness, and self-authorship.
Episode 01 dramatizes the Selemene `daily-practice` mirror: Biorhythm + Vedic Clock + Panchanga. The visual metaphor is a living arterial city where a child's nightmare appears as a rhythm interruption in the flow.

BOARD FORMAT
Make a single vertical flowboard image, similar in structure to the provided workflow references. Prefer a clean 4:5 or 2:3 vertical presentation-board composition, not a single cropped action still.

TOP HALF — CINEMATIC HERO KEYFRAME
A clean cinematic anime still of Pichet in a translucent arterial corridor beneath a living city. Pichet stands left-of-center, one open palm gently touching the arterial wall, calm and listening. The black-violet rhythm plaque appears on the right side of the wall. A soft golden dream-pocket suggesting Maya glows in the middle distance. Warm aged-gold plasma light, deep ink shadows, bone mist, restrained sacred geometry. The shot should feel clean, elegant, and story-first, not cluttered.

BOTTOM HALF — PRODUCTION FLOWBOARD PANELS
Create a clean organized set of panels below the hero image:
1. Pichet mini identity sheet: front, side, three-quarter head/torso views, mature worker expression.
2. Prop panel: one simple brass pressure gauge, one copper wrench, one repair chalk mark, shown separately with clean silhouettes.
3. Rhythm plaque panel: one black-violet plaque design with golden biorhythm rings, simple and readable.
4. Three small storyboard beats: Pichet listens to the arterial wall; plaque reveals the rhythm break; Maya's glow steadies as the flow clears.
5. Small color palette swatches: deep ink, bone mist, aged gold, terracotta, stone grey, black-violet.

MOTION-READINESS
The lower storyboard beats should be visually simple: one action per panel, no complex hand poses, no crowded mechanics. They should read as future animation frames, not decorative thumbnails.

LAYOUT QUALITY
Use the clean flowboard discipline of the references: strong panel grid, cinematic top image, lower design panels, clean margins, balanced spacing, clear silhouettes, minimal visual noise, professional animation pre-production board quality.

IMPORTANT TEXT RULE
No readable text. Do not include logos, fake brand names, pseudo-writing, labels, captions, UI, watermarks, or social-media marks. If the board needs annotation marks, use tiny abstract lines and non-readable sketch marks only.

HAND / ANATOMY CONTROL
Hands should be simple and low-risk. In the hero image, show Pichet's touching hand clearly as a relaxed open palm with normal fingers. Do not show complex bundled tools in the hand. Keep tools in separate lower prop panels.

STYLE
Premium original cinematic anime, clean studio presentation, soft painterly lighting, warm emotional atmosphere, polished pre-production board, subtle sacred geometry, restrained anatomical-city surrealism. Use clarity over maximal detail.

NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS
No cluttered background. No tangled micro-architecture. No tiny unreadable windows everywhere. No extra hands. No distorted fingers. No fused tools. No weaponized props. No forehead jewel unless from the Pichet identity reference. No young-boy Pichet. No logo. No readable words. No copied reference characters or settings. No direct imitation of any copyrighted anime studio or character.

FINAL GOAL
Make this feel like the first clean professional anime flowboard for TPoTHP: Pichet's mature identity, the arterial-city world, the rhythm-plaque problem, and Selemene's witness-first philosophy are all visible in one polished reference-style board.
```
