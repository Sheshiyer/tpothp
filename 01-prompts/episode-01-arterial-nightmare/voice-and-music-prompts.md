# Episode 01 — Voice + Music Prompts

## Pichet Voice Direction

```text
Voice role: Pichet, humble psychic plumber.
Voice quality: male, late 30s to early 40s, warm, grounded, practical, slightly tired but kind. He sounds like a tradesman who has seen mystical things and still remembers to clean his tools.

Performance direction:
Read calmly, without guru tone. He is not explaining doctrine; he is helping a frightened child understand what she is seeing.
Gentle pauses before important repair metaphors.
Keep the voice intimate and ordinary, with quiet wonder underneath.

Sample line:
"Nightmares are not attacks, Maya. Sometimes they are pipes carrying a truth that has nowhere else to go."

Direction for sample line:
Pause 0.4 seconds after "attacks".
Lower volume slightly on "truth".
End softly, as if listening to the city after speaking.

Settings:
Stability: 0.45
Similarity: 0.85
Style exaggeration: 0.25
Speaker boost: ON
```

## Maya Voice Direction

```text
Voice role: Maya, six-year-old child.
Voice quality: young, soft, frightened at first, then curious and relieved.
Performance direction:
Avoid cartoon exaggeration. The fear should feel real but gentle. Curiosity should arrive gradually.

Sample line:
"If it was scary... why is it shining?"

Direction:
Tiny pause after "scary".
The word "shining" should carry wonder, not fear.
```
